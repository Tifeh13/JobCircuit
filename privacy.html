Privacy Policy for CareerInTech

Last Updated: July 20, 2024

Introduction

Austeva (“we,” “our,” or “us”), located at 53 State Street Boston MA 02109, is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, job candidates, and client companies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website or use our staffing and recruiting services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from both job candidates and client companies. This information may include:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Physical addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Resumes
  • Work history
  • Information collected automatically when you visit our website

How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly to us when you:
    • Submit a job application
    • Register for our services
    • Communicate with us via email, phone, or other methods
  • Information collected automatically when you visit our website, through the use of cookies and similar technologies.

Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance user experience and collect information about how our site is used. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes including:

  • Providing and improving our staffing and recruiting services
  • Matching job candidates with potential employers
  • Communicating with you about our services
  • Analyzing and improving our website performance
  • Complying with legal obligations

Sharing of Information

We do not share or sell personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to object to certain processing of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the “Contact Us” section.

International Data Transfers

While we currently operate within the United States, we reserve the right to collect and process information from individuals located anywhere in the world. If we do so, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date.

California Privacy Rights

Data Retention

While we do not currently have specific data retention policies, we retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Consent

By using our website and services, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
